Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for a residential construction project?

The timeline varies based on the project size and complexity. A typical 2-3 BHK house takes 6-10 months from foundation to completion, including approvals and finishing work.

Do you provide interior design services for existing homes?

Yes, we offer complete interior design and renovation services for existing homes. Our team can transform your space with modern designs and quality materials.

What is included in your modular kitchen service?

Our modular kitchen service includes design consultation, custom cabinets, countertops, storage solutions, electrical work, plumbing, and installation of appliances.

Do you offer warranty on your construction work?

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranty on our construction work. Structural work comes with 5-year warranty, while interior work has 2-year warranty coverage.
